What is VEU?

The VEU program, administered by the Essential Services Commission (ESC), provides discounts and incentives for energy-efficient upgrades in Victorian homes and businesses. It creates Victorian Energy Efficiency Certificates (VEECs) for each tonne of greenhouse gas emissions saved.

Eligibility

All Victorian households and businesses qualify. No income limits for most rebates. Accredited providers like Suntech handle installations. Eligible activities include:

  • Solar PV systems (30-200kW)
  • Lighting upgrades
  • Heating/cooling systems (split systems)
  • Hot water systems
  • Insulation
  • Energy-efficient appliances

Systems must meet ESC standards; pre-accreditation installs are ineligible.

How VEU Works

VEU rewards energy savings with tradable VEECs (Victorian Energy Efficiency Certificates). Providers offer rebates by assigning VEECs to offset your system cost. The program has been extended to 2045, providing long-term savings opportunities.
