What is Solar Homes?

The Solar Homes Program offers rebates for solar panels, batteries, and hot water systems to Victorian households. Administered by Solar Victoria, the program aims to install 1 million solar systems by 2030.

Rebates Available

  • Solar Panels: $1,400 rebate
  • Solar Batteries: Up to $2,950 rebate (loans closed)
  • Solar Hot Water: Up to $1,000 rebate
  • Heat Pumps: Up to $1,000 rebate

Eligibility Requirements

‚úÖ Check Your Eligibility

  • Must be owner-occupier of the property
  • Property value less than $3 million
  • Combined household income less than $210,000/year
  • Existing homes or under construction
  • Haven't received a solar rebate before
  • Must use authorized retailer/installer (like Suntech)
  • For batteries: Must have existing solar or install simultaneously

How Solar Homes Works

The program provides state rebates to reduce upfront costs. When combined with federal STCs and VEU rebates, total savings can exceed $7,000.

Complete Solar Homes Process

  1. Check eligibility: Use the online tool on Solar Victoria website (requires ID and income proof)
  2. Get quote: Contact authorized retailer like Suntech for system quote
  3. Apply online: Via Service Victoria portal; upload quote, ID, and income documents
  4. Approval: Receive approval within days; rebate code issued
  5. Installation: Must be completed within 120 days by accredited team
  6. Claim rebate: We submit completion documentation; rebate paid directly to us (deducted from your cost)
  7. Monitor system: App access provided; warranty claims handled as needed
