Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our solar installation services in Bass Coast and across Victoria:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
  • Property details and energy usage information
  • Payment and billing information
  • Technical specifications and site assessment data
  • Communications and service records

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses:

  • Processing and fulfilling your solar installation service
  • Customer communications and ongoing support
  • System monitoring and maintenance services
  • Processing rebate applications (VEU, STC, Solar Homes)
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

Marketing: With your consent, we may send information about solar products, maintenance services, and energy efficiency tips. You can opt-out at any time by contacting us or using unsubscribe links.

Information Sharing

We share information only with trusted partners under strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, equipment suppliers, installation teams
  • Government Agencies: For rebate processing (ESC for VEU, Clean Energy Regulator for STCs, Solar Victoria for Solar Homes)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legitimate interests

VEU Program Data Sharing

Special Notice: For VEU rebate eligibility, we must share your site and energy data with the Essential Services Commission (ESC) and the VEU Registry. This requires your explicit consent and is governed by DEECA (Department of Energy, Environment and Climate Action) data policies.

Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure disposal of physical documents

Data Retention

Standard Retention: Customer information is retained for 7 years after service completion for warranty and legal compliance purposes.

VEU Data: VEU-related data is retained for 7 years post-installation as required by ESC regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updated policies will be posted on our website with the effective date. We will notify customers of material changes via email or other communication methods.

This policy is designed to comply with Australian privacy laws and VEU program requirements. For specific legal advice, consult with a qualified privacy professional.
